# AI-On-Earth

In creating this project, I collaborated with Claude (Sonnet 4.6) to assist with data collections and HTML development. I affirm that all AI-generated and co-created content underwent thorough review and evaluation. All architectural design, scope, and potential agentic use decisions were made entirely by me, with Claude executing on those decisions. No automated or agentic AI was used due to its failure to meet environmental, social, and governance standards. The final output accurately reflects my understanding, expertise, and intended meaning. While AI assistance was instrumental in the process, I maintain full responsibility for the content, its accuracy, and its presentation. AI-On-Earth is not intended to be used as a source of truth, but to realize the general impact of AI usage compared to other energy-dependent activities, personalized to one individual. This disclosure is made in the spirit of transparency and to acknowledge the role of AI in the creation process.
